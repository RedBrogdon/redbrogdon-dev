I met Rivers Cuomo once.

Rivers Cuomo, the lead singer/songwriter for the band Weezer, is also a legit app developer. Seriously, check his GitHub profile. He agreed to be a part of our livestream at the end of 2024 and not only brought some really good questions but was exactly as nice in real life as you'd hope.

At the end of the shoot, a security guard who'd previously been working in the studio suddenly reappeared with a guitar case and an armful of LPs. He told Rivers that his son was the biggest Weezer fan in the world and asked if he'd sign something. Rivers took a good five minutes to autograph every record and drew a Weezer logo onto the guitar.

He also did a great job trying to make the jokes I'd written for us land well, which is 90% of what I look for in a co-presenter. ðŸ˜‚
